Fees

Earthly Roads allow you to choose when to, and how to pay for our rental services.

We have three ways to pay: daily, weekly, and monthly. These are changeable at any time during your rental.

We accept cash, checks, debit, credit, and bitcoin. Your payment type is changeable at any time during your rental.
