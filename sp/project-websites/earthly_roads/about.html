About Us

The earth needs us to be more concious about our actions and how they effect it. We need to be more diligent in reducing the damage we cause on earth and we’re here to help with that.

Earthly Roads is an ongoing American success story. Our guiding principles, and humble beginning, revolve around not only saving the earth and reducing the damage but also making it less expensive to do so. We believe in strengthening our communities one neighborhood at a time, serving our customers, and rewarding their eco consciousness.

Today, our massive network means Earthly Roads is the largest eco-friendly transportation solutions provider. We offer eco-friendly car and truck rentals, as well as car sharing and car sales. We're there when you need us with over 700 locations across the east coast.

Earthly Roads is now a household name for frequent eco-friendly travelers, road trippers and those with a car in the shop. We're a brand that’s recognized as a the east coast’s leader in the eco-friendly car rental industry. We value employees and customers as much as we value the earth, keeping it sustainable and healthy.

We take an active role in sustainability, not only because it’s smart for our business, but because we believe in making the world a better place for future generations. Because of our size, we are in a unique position to foster innovation, advance research and test market-driven solutions.
