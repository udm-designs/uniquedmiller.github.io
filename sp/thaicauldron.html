The Thai Cauldron

Tea Shop | Print & Web

The Thai Cauldron is a local Orlando tea shop with an occult theme. They specialize in organic loose leaf teas, sustainability, and allowing consumers to create their own unique tea mixes which they sometimes dub as brews or concoctions. We went for a modern, dark theme and utilized two rich tea colors.
