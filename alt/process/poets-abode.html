Poet's Abode

UX Design | Conceptual App

Poet's Abode is a conceptual social hobby app designed to fill the peculiar niche of a community based poetry application where users can create small communities to share their poetry with.

The Problem or Prompt

As a poet that frequents various social media apps, I noticed that most of the social media apps were not well formatted or designed for poetry while most of the poetry apps were heavily lacking in the community aspect or it's user experience.

Primary Pain Points:

  • Lack of or badly implemented social features.
  • Difficult or unfriendly user interface.
  • Inefficient interface for posting and viewing poetry.
  • Lack of privacy or control over who can and cannot view your poetry.

Hypothesis and Suggestions

To address the issues above, I hypothesized that there would need to be an app that revolved around the close-knit communities and writing. By focusing on these two features as the primary features, we could create an app that acts as a digital writer's club for poetry.

Top Priorities:

  • Friendly and intuitive UI for a seamless UX.
  • Community and social related features with control for privacy.
  • In-app word processor to make for a user-friendly process with good text and paragraph customization options when creating a poem.
  • Privacy and viewership options before posting poem with options to change after poem has been posted as well.

Research

After my initial impressions, I decided to do more research. I conducted user testing with 8 users where users were instructed to do three main tasks across two apps: post a poem, perform a social interaction, join a group or community, and edit the visibility options of your posted poem.
